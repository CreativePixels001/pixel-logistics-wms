## 🎤 Voice Input with Indian Languages

### How to Use
1. Click microphone button in editor
2. Select language from dropdown (next to mic button)
3. Click mic again to start recording
4. Speak in your chosen language
5. Click to stop

### Supported Languages
1. English (Indian)
2. Hindi (हिंदी)
3. Tamil (தமிழ்)
4. Telugu (తెలుగు)
5. Marathi (मराठी)
6. Bengali (বাংলা)
7. Gujarati (ગુજરાતી)
8. Kannada (ಕನ್ನಡ)
9. Malayalam (മലയാളം)
10. Punjabi (ਪੰਜਾਬੀ)

### Tips
- Speak clearly for best accuracy
- Language preference is saved automatically
- Works best in Chrome/Edge/Safari
- Requires internet connection

## 📤 Share PI Suggestions

### 3 Ways to Share

**1. Copy to Clipboard**
- Click green "Share" button in Column 3
- Click "Copy to Clipboard"
- Paste anywhere (email, chat, docs)

**2. Download as Text**
- Click green "Share" button
- Click "Download as Text"
- Gets saved as `[NoteName].txt`

**3. Save as Template**
- Click gray "Save" button in Column 3
- Enter template name
- Reuse later (future feature: template menu)

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### Voice input not working?
- Check browser (Chrome/Edge/Safari only)
- Allow microphone permissions
- Ensure internet connection
- Try different language

### PI not generating?
- Write at least 10 words
- Wait 2-3 seconds
- Check Column 3 for "PI is analyzing..."

### Share button disabled?
- Need to have PI content first
- Write something in Column 2
- Wait for PI to generate
