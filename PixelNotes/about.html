About PixelNotes

We're on a mission to transform how teams capture, organize, and leverage knowledge in the modern workplace

Our Story

PixelNotes was born from a simple observation: teams waste countless hours searching for information, rewriting notes, and struggling to maintain institutional knowledge. Despite having powerful collaboration tools, the fundamental challenge of capturing and finding information remained unsolved.

We founded PixelNotes in 2024 with a vision to create the world's most intelligent note-taking platform. Not just another digital notebook, but a system that learns, suggests, and actively helps teams work smarter.

"We believe that knowledge should flow freely within organizations, accessible to everyone who needs it, when they need it."

Our team combines expertise in artificial intelligence, enterprise software, and productivity tools. We've experienced firsthand the frustration of lost knowledge and inefficient workflows. That's why we built PixelNotes with a relentless focus on three principles: intelligence, simplicity, and security.

Today, PixelNotes serves over 500 enterprise teams across 25 countries, helping them capture over 10 million notes and saving thousands of hours in productivity. But we're just getting started.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we build

Intelligence First

We believe AI should enhance human intelligence, not replace it. Our PI assistant learns from you, suggests helpful insights, and makes your notes smarter without getting in your way. Every feature is designed to make you more productive.

Simplicity Always

Powerful doesn't have to mean complicated. We obsess over every interaction, removing friction and making complex features feel intuitive. If a feature requires training, we haven't designed it well enough.

Security by Default

Your notes contain your most valuable ideas and sensitive information. We treat security as a fundamental requirement, not an afterthought. Enterprise-grade encryption, compliance, and privacy are built into every layer.

Customer Success

We succeed only when our customers succeed. Every decision is evaluated through the lens of customer impact. We listen, iterate quickly, and maintain the highest standards of support and service.
