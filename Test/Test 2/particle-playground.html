📖 Quick Guide ▼

Hand Gestures:

  • ✋ Open hands → Particles expand
  • ✊ Close fists → Particles contract
  • ↔️ Move hands apart/together → Scale particles
  • 👏 Quick clap → Fireworks burst

Mouse/Touch Controls:

  • 🖱️ Move mouse → Attract particles
  • 🖱️ Click & drag → Manual control
  • 📱 Touch & drag → Same on mobile

Performance Tips:

  • Lower particle count on slower devices
  • Disable camera gestures for better FPS
  • Use GPU-accelerated browser
