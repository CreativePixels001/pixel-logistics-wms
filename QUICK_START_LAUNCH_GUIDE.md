## ðŸ’¡ DEMO TIPS (CRITICAL FOR SUCCESS)

### Before the Demo

**1. Qualify the Prospect (5 min pre-call)**
- Current WMS or system?
- Warehouse size?
- Pain points?
- Decision-making process?
- Budget/timeline?

**2. Prepare Custom Demo**
- Use their industry terminology
- Show relevant workflows
- Prepare specific examples
- Have answers ready for common objections

### During the Demo

**1. Start Strong (First 2 Minutes)**
```
"Thanks for joining! Before we dive in, quick question:
What's your biggest challenge with [current system/process]?

[Listen, then...]

Perfect - I'll show you exactly how Pixel Logistics solves that.
We've helped similar [industry] companies reduce [pain point] by 30-40%."
```

**2. Follow the STAR Pattern**
- **Situation:** "Currently you're using..."
- **Task:** "You need to..."
- **Action:** "Here's how our system handles that..."
- **Result:** "Which means you'll see..."

**3. Handle Objections**

**Objection 1:** "We need to see it with our data"
- **Answer:** "Great! We offer a 30-day pilot. You can import sample data and test with your team. Zero risk."

**Objection 2:** "What about integrations?"
- **Answer:** "We have a REST API ready. Most ERP integrations take 2-4 weeks. Which systems do you use?"

**Objection 3:** "How is this different from [competitor]?"
- **Answer:** "Three key differences: 1) Price (95% cheaper), 2) Speed (8 weeks vs 12 months), 3) UX (modern vs legacy). Want to see the comparison?"

**Objection 4:** "We need to think about it"
- **Answer:** "Totally understand. What specific concerns can I address? Also, would a 30-day free trial help you evaluate it risk-free?"

### After the Demo

**1. Immediate Follow-Up (Within 1 Hour)**
```
Subject: Thanks for your time - Next Steps

Hi [Name],

1. [Specific action item from call]

Best,
[Name]
```
