Introduction to PixelAudit

PixelAudit is a mobile-first audit management platform designed to streamline your entire audit workflow - from assignment to reporting.

Key Features

  • 📱 No-app mobile audit forms (works in browser)
  • 💬 WhatsApp/SMS notifications for instant assignment
  • 📸 Photo capture with offline support
  • 📄 Auto-generated PDF reports
  • 📊 Real-time dashboard and analytics
  • 💰 Simple pay-as-you-grow pricing (₹1, ₹100, ₹1,000)

Quick Start Guide

1

Sign Up with Google

Click "Start Free Trial" and sign in with your Google account. No credit card required.

2

Choose a Template

Select from pre-built templates (Restaurant Safety, Warehouse Compliance, Retail Store) or create your own.

3

Assign an Audit

Enter client details and auditor's phone number. They'll receive a WhatsApp link instantly.

4

Review & Export

Once completed, review the audit on your dashboard and download a professional PDF report.

Pro Tip: Your 1-hour free trial starts when you sign up. Make the most of it by assigning a real audit to test the full workflow!

Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:

  • Quick Stats: Total audits, pending, completed, and active clients
  • Category Cards: Organize audits by type (Travel, Logistics, Retail, etc.)
  • Recent Activity: Latest audit completions and assignments
  • Trial Timer: Track remaining time in your free trial
