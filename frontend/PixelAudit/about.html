About PixelAudit

Smart and Pixel Perfect Audit Management Platform

Our Mission

PixelAudit was born from a simple observation: small audit firms struggle with manual Excel sheets and WhatsApp chaos while expensive enterprise software is out of reach. We're building a mobile-first audit platform that's accessible to everyone.

Why We Built This

After talking to hundreds of audit firms, we discovered the same pain points:

  • Manual data entry takes hours every day
  • Coordinating auditors via WhatsApp is chaotic
  • Creating professional PDF reports is time-consuming
  • Expensive software requires long-term contracts

Our Solution

PixelAudit is designed to be ridiculously simple: assign audits via WhatsApp in 30 seconds, field auditors complete checklists on their phones (no app download needed), and admins review with auto-generated professional PDF reports. Start with just ₹1 for lifetime access.

Built by Auditors, for Auditors

Our founder, Ashish Kumar, spent months working alongside audit firms like Vishal's, understanding every pain point. Every feature in PixelAudit solves a real problem we witnessed firsthand.
