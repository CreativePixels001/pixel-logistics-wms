Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and email address (via Google OAuth)
  • Phone numbers of auditors you assign
  • Client information (names, addresses, contact details)
  • Audit data (checklists, photos, notes, reports)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Razorpay)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our audit management services
  • To send WhatsApp/SMS notifications for audit assignments
  • To generate PDF reports for your clients
  • To process payments and maintain billing records
  • To send important product updates and security alerts
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform

3. Data Security

We take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Photos are stored securely on AWS S3 with restricted access. We implement industry-standard security practices including regular backups, access controls, and monitoring.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We only share data with:

  • Twilio (WhatsApp/SMS delivery)
  • Razorpay (payment processing)
  • AWS (cloud hosting and storage)
  • SendGrid (email delivery)

All third-party services are GDPR and data protection compliant.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your data at any time. You can export all your audit data, download reports, and delete your account from the Settings page. For any privacy concerns, contact us at privacy@pixelaudit.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your audit data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all data will be permanently deleted within 30 days, except for financial records required by law (retained for 7 years).

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email. Continued use of PixelAudit after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
