Frequently Asked Questions

How do I assign an audit to a field auditor?

Click "Assign Audit" from the dashboard, select a client and template, enter the auditor's phone number, and click send. They'll receive a WhatsApp/SMS with a unique link that works without login or app download.

Do field auditors need to install an app?

No! Audits work directly in the mobile browser. Auditors just click the WhatsApp link and start filling the checklist immediately.

What happens when I reach my audit limit?

You'll see a notification when approaching your limit. Simply upgrade to the next tier to continue. All your existing data stays intact.

Can I customize the PDF report design?

The ₹1,000 Enterprise tier includes white-label reports with your logo and branding. Lower tiers use the standard PixelAudit template.
