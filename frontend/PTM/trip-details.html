Trip Overview

Experience the grandeur of Rajasthan with this carefully curated 7-day journey through the land of kings and queens. Visit majestic forts, opulent palaces, and vibrant bazaars. Explore the pink city of Jaipur, the blue city of Jodhpur, and the romantic lake city of Udaipur. Immerse yourself in rich Rajasthani culture, savor authentic cuisine, and witness traditional art forms. This trip combines UNESCO World Heritage Sites, cultural experiences, and comfortable accommodations for an unforgettable royal experience.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

1
Arrival in Jaipur - The Pink City
Arrive at Jaipur International Airport. Transfer to hotel and check-in. Evening visit to Birla Temple and local markets. Enjoy welcome dinner with traditional Rajasthani folk music and dance performance. Overnight stay in Jaipur.
2
Jaipur Sightseeing - Amber Fort & City Palace
Morning visit to magnificent Amber Fort with elephant ride (optional). Explore City Palace, Jantar Mantar observatory (UNESCO Site), and iconic Hawa Mahal. Visit local handicraft workshops. Evening free for shopping at Johari Bazaar. Overnight in Jaipur.
3
Jaipur to Jodhpur - Journey to the Blue City
After breakfast, drive to Jodhpur (335 km, 6 hours). En route visit Pushkar (optional). Check-in at hotel. Evening visit to Clock Tower and Sardar Market. Enjoy sunset view from city viewpoint. Overnight in Jodhpur.
4
Jodhpur Exploration - Mehrangarh Fort
Full day exploring Jodhpur. Visit the imposing Mehrangarh Fort, one of India's largest forts with museum and galleries. Explore Jaswant Thada marble cenotaph, Umaid Bhawan Palace. Walk through the blue painted houses of old city. Enjoy traditional Rajasthani dinner. Overnight in Jodhpur.
5
Jodhpur to Udaipur - The City of Lakes
Morning drive to Udaipur (255 km, 5 hours). En route visit Ranakpur Jain Temples with intricate marble carvings. Arrive in Udaipur and check-in at hotel. Evening boat ride on Lake Pichola. View City Palace illuminated at night. Overnight in Udaipur.
6
Udaipur Sightseeing - Palaces & Gardens
Full day Udaipur tour. Visit magnificent City Palace overlooking Lake Pichola, Jagdish Temple, Saheliyon ki Bari gardens. Optional visit to vintage car museum. Evening cultural show at Bagore ki Haveli. Farewell dinner at rooftop restaurant with lake view. Overnight in Udaipur.
7
Departure from Udaipur
After breakfast, check-out from hotel. Optional morning visit to Eklingji Temple. Transfer to Udaipur Airport for your onward journey with wonderful memories of Royal Rajasthan.
