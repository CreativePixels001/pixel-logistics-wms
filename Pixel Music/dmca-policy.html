Last Updated: December 17, 2025

1. Copyright Commitment

Creative Pixels respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users of Pixel Play to do the same. We comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and other applicable copyright laws.

⚠️ Important Notice: Pixel Play does not host, store, or distribute copyrighted music files. All YouTube content is streamed directly from YouTube's servers and is subject to YouTube's copyright policies.

2. How Pixel Play Works

2.1 YouTube Integration

Pixel Play uses YouTube's official API to search for and stream music videos. We do not:

  • Download or store YouTube content on our servers
  • Enable users to download YouTube videos
  • Modify or redistribute YouTube content
  • Bypass YouTube's copyright protection measures

All YouTube content remains on YouTube's platform and is subject to YouTube's copyright policies and Content ID system.

2.2 Local File Playback

Users can upload and play their own audio files. These files:

  • Are stored locally on the user's device only
  • Are not uploaded to our servers
  • Are not shared with other users
  • Remain the user's sole responsibility

3. User Responsibilities

3.1 Respect Copyright

By using Pixel Play, you agree to:

  • Only upload audio files you own or have permission to use

3.2 YouTube Content Guidelines

When accessing YouTube content through Pixel Play:

  • You must comply with all YouTube policies
  • Content is for personal, non-commercial streaming only
  • You may not circumvent YouTube's ads or monetization
  • You may not download or save YouTube videos

4. DMCA Takedown Procedure

4.1 Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe your copyrighted work has been infringed through Pixel Play, please note:

For YouTube Content:

We do not host YouTube content. To report copyright infringement of YouTube videos, please use YouTube's official DMCA process: YouTube Copyright Complaint Form

For Our Platform:

If you believe Pixel Play's code, design, or content infringes your copyright, please send a DMCA notice to our designated agent with the following information:

4.2 Required Information for DMCA Notice

Your DMCA notice must include:

  1. Your Signature: Physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized agent
  2. Identification of Work: Description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed
  3. Location of Material: URL or specific location of the allegedly infringing material
  4. Your Contact Information: Name, address, telephone number, and email address
  5. Good Faith Statement: A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  6. Accuracy Statement: A statement under penalty of perjury that the information is accurate and you are authorized to act

4.3 Send DMCA Notice To:

DMCA Designated Agent:
Creative Pixels
Attention: Copyright Agent
Email: dmca@creativepixels.in
Subject Line: "DMCA Takedown Request"

5. Counter-Notification

If you believe content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification with:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature
  2. Identification of the removed material and its prior location
  3. A statement under penalty of perjury that removal was a mistake or misidentification
  4. Your name, address, phone number, and consent to jurisdiction

Send counter-notifications to the same address above with subject line "DMCA Counter-Notification".

6. Repeat Infringer Policy

In accordance with the DMCA and other applicable laws, we have adopted a policy of terminating accounts of users who are repeat infringers. We may terminate access for users who:

  • Repeatedly upload copyrighted material without authorization
  • Receive multiple valid DMCA complaints
  • Attempt to circumvent copyright protection measures

7. Fair Use

We recognize that some uses of copyrighted material may qualify as fair use under copyright law. Fair use is a legal doctrine that permits limited use of copyrighted material without permission for purposes such as:

  • Commentary and criticism
  • News reporting
  • Teaching and education
  • Research and scholarship

However, Pixel Play is primarily a music streaming service for personal entertainment, which generally does not qualify for fair use protection.

8. YouTube Content ID

  • Copyright owners receive appropriate credit and/or revenue
  • Blocked or restricted videos remain inaccessible
  • Regional restrictions are enforced
