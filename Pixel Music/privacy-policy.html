Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pixel Play ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our music streaming application.

By using Pixel Play, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you choose to sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Name: Your full name from your Google account
  • Email Address: Your Google email address
  • Profile Picture: Your Google profile picture URL
  • Google ID: Your unique Google user identifier

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our service:

  • Search Queries: Songs and artists you search for
  • Playback Data: Songs played, skip actions, and playback duration
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • Local Storage Data: Session information stored in your browser

2.3 Third-Party Data

We integrate with third-party services that may collect data:

  • YouTube: Video IDs, search results, and playback events
  • Google OAuth: Authentication credentials and user profile
  • Lyrics.ovh: Song title and artist for lyrics fetching

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and manage your user account
  • Service Delivery: To provide music streaming and playback features
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences and session state
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our service
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and fraud

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

We store user session data in your browser's localStorage, including your name, email, and login type. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers except during authentication.

4.2 Service Worker Cache

Our service worker caches application files locally on your device for offline functionality and improved performance. No personal data is stored in the cache.

4.3 Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Google OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • No server-side storage of personal data
  • Client-side data protection through browser security
Important: While we implement security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Services

We use Google Sign-In and YouTube API. Your use of these services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 YouTube API Services

By using Pixel Play, you are also agreeing to be bound by YouTube's Terms of Service: https://www.youtube.com/t/terms

5.3 Lyrics.ovh

We use Lyrics.ovh API to fetch song lyrics. Song titles and artists are sent to their service. Review their privacy policy at their website.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following technologies:

  • localStorage: To maintain user session and preferences
  • Service Worker: For offline functionality and caching
  • Third-Party Cookies: Google and YouTube may set their own cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit functionality of Pixel Play.

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we store about you
  • Correction: Update your personal information through Google account settings
  • Deletion: Clear your data by logging out and clearing browser storage
  • Opt-Out: Use Guest Mode to avoid providing personal information

7.2 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights:

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority

7.3 CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information

Note: We do not sell your personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Pixel Play is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to request deletion.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

  • Session Data: Stored in localStorage until you log out or clear browser data
  • Cached Files: Stored until you clear browser cache or we update the service worker
  • Google Authentication: Managed by Google's retention policies

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Continued use of Pixel Play after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
